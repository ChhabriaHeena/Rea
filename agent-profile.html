Charlotte Robbins

Sales Consultant with MHK

10 Reviews

“I love the excitement of something new, making people happy and building relationships” - Charlotte Robbins

Hailing originally from the UK and having spent many years travelling, Charlotte joined the Morton & Morton team in 2011 as a Sales Associate in our Circular Quay office. Working alongside Ettiene West, one of our leading sales agents, Charlotte’s previous experience with a well known UK based real estate company has proven invaluable.

Having been led to Morton & Morton through excellent word of mouth, Charlotte not only thrives in her challenging role, she excels in it.

As someone who appreciates great food and wine, Charlotte particularly enjoys working in our Circular Quay office where she is literally surrounded by some of Sydney’s best restaurants and bars. Charlotte also loves spending time with her husband and friends, taking long walks and when time permits, travelling.
